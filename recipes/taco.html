Taco Recipe

Description

Tacos are a versatile and delicious Mexican staple. This recipe uses seasoned ground beef tucked into warm shells and topped with fresh ingredients. It's the perfect quick meal for a busy weeknight.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 packet Taco Seasoning
  • 12 Taco Shells (hard or soft)
  • 1 cup Shredded Lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Diced Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream and Salsa for topping

Steps

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until it is no longer pink. Drain off the excess fat.
  2. Season: Stir in the taco seasoning and a small splash of water. Simmer for about 5 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  3. Warm the Shells: Heat your taco shells in the oven or microwave according to the package directions to make them pliable or crispy.
  4. Assemble: Spoon a few tablespoons of the beef mixture into each shell.
  5. Top it Off: Add your lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese. Garnish with a dollop of sour cream and your favorite salsa.
