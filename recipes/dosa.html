Dosa recipe

A classic South Indian breakfast! This thin, fermented crepe is made from rice and lentil batter, typically served with spicy coconut chutney, and sambar.


Ingredients

  • 3 cups Parboiled Rice
  • 1 cup Urad Dal (split black lentils)
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or Ghee for cooking

Steps

  1. Soak & Grind: Soak rice and dal separately for 6 hours. Grind into a smooth paste and mix together.
  2. Ferment: Let the batter sit in a warm place for 8-12 hours until it rises and smells slightly tangy.
  3. Prepare the Pan: Heat a non-stick tawa and lightly grease it with oil.
  4. Spread: Pour a ladle of batter in the center and spread it in a wide circular motion to make it thin.
  5. Cook: Drizzle oil around the edges. Cook until the bottom is golden brown and crispy.
