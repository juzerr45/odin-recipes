Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food. Layers of meaty sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella come together for the perfect family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef until browned. Stir in tomatoes and paste; simmer for 1.5 hours.
  2. Prepare the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente.
  3. Mix the cheese: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with one egg and a pinch of salt.
  4. Layer the lasagna: Spread meat sauce in a baking dish, then layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
